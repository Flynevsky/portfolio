Sixth setup,
From december 2021 to march 2022.

Telescope
Dobson Kepler 300/1500 GSO
A new big telescope with an extremly accurate resolution. I bought it for 700€ (it costs ~1100€ new) in December. It has a resolving capacity of 0.38", a limit value (mag) of 14.2 and a light gathering capacity of 1840. The tube weighs 19.5kg alone, 20kg fully equipped. I bought it with two eyepieces and a laser.


Mount
Equatorial Skywatcher NEQ6 Pro
A basic dobson mount made of wood that weighs 13.5kg.


Imagers
Redmi note 10 5G
It is a dobson mount so that would be very hard to take photos with it, I tried to, but my planetary camera was inapproriate as it zooms too much, so I tried with my phone. I also used my phone to take photos of the sky.




I kept my Skywatcher 150/1200 and my old broken mount, the size difference was very impressing. Also the weight difference is huge, 33kg for the Kepler and ~17kg for the Skywatcher.




The size of tubes was also important.
