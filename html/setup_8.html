It comes with a great focuser with micro-focusing which is perfect for planetary observations. This beast weighs 12 kgs which is close to my NEQ-6 Pro max payload. Mount
Equatorial Skywatcher NEQ6 Pro
The same mount, a very precise equatorial mount that I exchanged for my Kepler 300/1500. This equatorial mount track stars by compensating the Earth rotation so I can take photos with long exposure, and so the imager captures more light. The payload capacity is 18 kg and the total weight (with counterweight (2x5kg), without telescope) is 33.5kg. The resolution is 0.144 arc sec, the positioning accuracy is up to 1 arc minute and the mount compensates the mechanical errors. It featured an ST-4 Auto Guider Interface for auto guidance camera, my ASI 120MC-S Color made the job.


Imager
Nikon D5200
An old DSLR camera, it really changed the quality of my photos. It has a resolution of 6000x4000.


Autoguiding
ASI 120 MC-S Color
Using my Nikon D5200 as the imager allowed me to use autoguiding, the ASI 120 MC-S Color greatly made the job.
